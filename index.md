# KeySense

Offline music **key**, **BPM**, and **Camelot** analysis for Android.

KeySense analyzes music entirely on your device — no uploads, no accounts, no tracking.

- **Key detection** — 24-key major/minor with KS + Temperley dual-profile correlation
- **Tempo & meter** — BeatNet-powered ONNX inference with conservative octave correction
- **Camelot wheel** — Mixed-In-Key compatible visualization for DJs and producers
- **Privacy-first** — raw audio is never uploaded or saved
- **Material You** — adaptive Material 3 theming, light + dark
